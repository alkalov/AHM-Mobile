How to Use

Sim Tab

Configure your strategy across five sections — Core, Risk, Ticker, Psychology, Overrides. Toggle fields ON/OFF. Hit SIMULATE. Edge diagnostics and equity curve appear below.

Table Tab

Day-by-day simulation breakdown. Adjust the interval stepper to control row density. Scrolls horizontally for full data.

Session Tab

Log each trading day after the close. After several entries, a baseline emerges and grades unlock.

Journal Tab

Track sleep, diet, stress, fatigue and emotional state daily. Sparklines appear after 3+ entries showing trends over time.

Stats Tab

Enter broker stats to compare against your simulation targets in real time.

Key Terms

  • Tick — $0.01/share (US equities)
  • R:R — Reward:Risk ratio
  • Edge — Expected profit per trade after friction
  • Pullback Kill — Win that reverses past target, becomes a loss
  • Friction — Commission + slippage + spread per trade
