How to Use

Sim

Configure your strategy across five sections. Toggle fields ON/OFF. Hit Simulate. Chart, diagnostics and results appear inline below.

Table

Day-by-day breakdown. Adjust the interval stepper. Scrolls horizontally for all columns.

Log

Top half: log today's trades after the close. Grades unlock after a baseline forms. Bottom half: journal your mental and physical state daily.

Stats

Enter real broker data to compare against your simulation targets in real time.

Key Terms

  • Tick — $0.01/share (US equities)
  • R:R — Reward:Risk ratio
  • Edge — Expected profit after all costs
  • Pullback Kill — Win that reverses past target, becomes a loss
